Diseases & Symptoms

This listing helps you understand the diseases we treat and their symptoms so you can take early action.

Hijama Therapy

Needed when the body shows signs of toxin buildup, chronic pain, fatigue, poor blood circulation, or inflammation. It is used for conditions like migraines, joint pain, back pain, high blood pressure, stress, and digestive issues. Hijama is believed to remove stagnant blood, boost immunity, and restore natural energy flow, making it a preventive and healing therapy in both traditional and prophetic medicine.

Liver Problems

Fatigue, abdominal discomfort, and yellowing of the skin or eyes (Jaundice). Conditions like Fatty Liver, Hepatomegaly, and Hepatitis (alcoholic or non-alcoholic) may lead to nausea and loss of appetite. In severe cases, symptoms may include Recurrent Jaundice, fluid buildup in the abdomen (Ascites), and signs of Liver Cirrhosis like swelling or confusion.

Sciatica

Sharp or burning pain that radiates from the lower back to the leg, numbness, tingling, muscle weakness, and a pins-and-needles sensation, usually affecting one side of the body. It may worsen with sitting, standing, or sudden movements.

Migrane

Throbbing or pulsating headache, usually on one side of the head. It may be accompanied by nausea, vomiting, and sensitivity to light, sound, or smells. Some people experience visual disturbances called auras, such as flashing lights or blind spots, before the headache starts. Migraines can last from a few hours to several days and may worsen with physical activity.

Joints Problems

Pain, stiffness, swelling, and limited movement. Conditions like Osteoarthritis, Rheumatoid Arthritis, Gout, Sciatica, Lumbar Spondylosis, Cervical Spondylosis, Frozen Shoulder often lead to inflammation, discomfort, and reduced flexibility in affected areas.

Sexual Health Issues

Erectile Dysfunction(ED)- weak or no erection, Premature Ejaculation(PE)- ejaculation too early during intimacy, Low Sperm Count(Oligospermia)- difficulty in conceiving, Lack of stamina, Low Desire or Fatigue, Performance anxiety or low confidence in married life.
